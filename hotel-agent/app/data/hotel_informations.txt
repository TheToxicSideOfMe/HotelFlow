# HOTEL KNOWLEDGE BASE — AZURE GRAND HOTEL

---

## GENERAL INFORMATION

Hotel Name: Azure Grand Hotel
Location: 14 Boulevard de la Corniche, Tunis, Tunisia
Phone: +216 71 000 000
Email: contact@azuregrand.tn
Website: www.azuregrand.tn
Star Rating: 4-star hotel
Languages spoken at reception: Arabic, French, English

The Azure Grand Hotel is a modern 4-star hotel located on the scenic Corniche Boulevard in Tunis, just minutes from the city center and a short drive from Carthage-Tunis International Airport. The hotel combines contemporary design with traditional Tunisian hospitality, offering guests a comfortable and elegant experience whether they are visiting for business or leisure.

## CHECK-IN AND CHECK-OUT

Check-in time: 2:00 PM
Check-out time: 12:00 PM (noon)

Late check-out: Available upon request until 3:00 PM for an additional fee of 40 TND. After 3:00 PM, a full night is charged.

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

**Q: Is Wi-Fi free?**
A: Yes, high-speed Wi-Fi is complimentary in all rooms and common areas.

**Q: Is parking available?**
A: Yes, the hotel offers free on-site parking for all guests.

**Q: Can I request a specific floor or room?**
A: Special requests can be noted at the time of booking and the hotel will do its best to accommodate them, but they cannot be guaranteed.

**Q: Do you offer airport transfers?**
A: Yes, airport transfers can be arranged through the concierge. The fee is 60 TND one way or 100 TND round trip. Booking must be made at least 24 hours in advance.

**Q: Is breakfast included?**
A: Breakfast is included in the Junior Suite rate and some promotional packages. For all other room types, breakfast can be added for 25 TND (continental) or 40 TND (full buffet) per person.

**Q: Do you have a pool?**
A: Yes, the hotel has both an outdoor pool (open May to October) and an indoor heated pool (open year-round).

**Q: Can I store luggage after check-out?**
A: Yes, complimentary luggage storage is available at the front desk.

**Q: Are children welcome?**
A: Yes, children of all ages are welcome. Children under 6 stay free when using existing bedding. A baby cot can be provided on request at no charge.

**Q: Is the hotel suitable for business travelers?**
A: Yes, the hotel has a fully equipped business center, high-speed Wi-Fi, and meeting rooms for groups of 10 to 80 people. Corporate rates are available.

**Q: What is the currency used?**
A: The hotel accepts Tunisian Dinar (TND), Visa, and Mastercard. Prices are listed in TND and include VAT.

**Q: Do you have a spa?**
A: Yes, the hotel has a full spa and wellness center offering massages, hammam, and facial treatments. Open daily from 9:00 AM to 8:00 PM. Advance booking is recommended.

**Q: Is the hotel close to tourist attractions?**
A: Yes. The Medina of Tunis is 4 km away, Sidi Bou Said is 15 km, and Carthage ruins are approximately 10 km from the hotel. The concierge can help arrange tours and excursions.

**Q: What is the check-in age requirement?**
A: Guests must be at least 18 years old with a valid ID or passport to check in.

**Q: Can I modify my booking?**
A: Yes, bookings can be modified subject to availability. Please contact the hotel or use the booking portal. Changes within 48 hours of check-in may be subject to fees.
